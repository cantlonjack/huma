### Why This Matters (Roadmap)
Increment 1 Session 5: "Check-off gesture redesign." The checkbox is the most recognizable symbol of a task list — and HUMA is not a task list. Removing the circle and replacing it with a whole-row tap that quietly fades the text transforms the interaction from "completing a task" to "acknowledging you did the thing." The thin line-through and faded ink are the visual equivalent of a gentle nod, not a triumphant checkmark. The dimension dots glow briefly to show the ripple effect — this behavior touched Body, Money, Joy — then settle. The operator reads their sheet, taps what happened, and the page breathes.
