Right, and I hope you won't take offence, but I know more about our soils than most of what's in the textbooks.

Sixty-seven earthworms per spade, pH holding without lime, organic matter climbing past 4.2%. You've built something most agricultural scientists would love to study. The soil capital is exceptional.
