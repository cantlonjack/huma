# HUMA — The Real Product Design
## First Principles, Not Shortcuts

*The previous "product critique" correctly identified the problem 
(40 minutes is too much upfront commitment) but proposed the wrong 
solution ("What's on your mind?" is just ChatGPT with better framing). 
This document starts from what actually produces the coherence 
recognition moment and works backwards to the product.*

---

## THE PROBLEM WITH "WHAT'S ON YOUR MIND?"

One paragraph of text gives HUMA access to ONE slice of someone's 
life — whatever they're consciously worried about. But HUMA's entire 
value is seeing connections ACROSS dimensions that the person CAN'T 
see. You can't find the connection between money stress and sleep 
quality from a single paragraph about money stress. You need the 
whole picture.

ChatGPT can respond to "I can't sleep, money is tight" with decent 
advice. So can any therapist, friend, or self-help book. The response 
will be generic: "financial stress affects sleep quality, try writing 
down your worries." That's not insight. That's trivia.

A REAL insight requires seeing the SHAPE — the relationship between 
ALL dimensions simultaneously. "Your Money and Body are both low, 
but your People and Identity are unusually strong for someone in 
financial stress. Most people in your situation feel isolated. 
You don't. That social strength isn't just nice to have — it's 
your most powerful lever. The question isn't how to make more money. 
It's how to let the people around you support what's struggling."

That's specific. That's structural. That's something only HUMA can 
see, because only HUMA has the full dimensional picture. And it 
requires data across ALL 8 dimensions — not just the one they're 
conscious of.

So the first experience must capture the WHOLE SHAPE — quickly, 
naturally, and without feeling like a medical intake form.

---

## THE SHAPE BUILDER (Day 1, 90 seconds)

### What it is

The operator's first experience with HUMA is not typing. It's 
SHAPING. They build a picture of their life by responding to 8 
questions — one per dimension — in a visual, tactile, 60-90 second 
interaction.

### What it feels like

Not a questionnaire. Not sliders. Not "rate your financial wellness 
from 1-5." It should feel like looking in a mirror you've never 
seen before.

### How it works

Eight cards, presented one at a time (swipeable, or tap to advance). 
Each card is one dimension, but the dimension NAME is not the focus — 
the QUESTION is.

```
Card 1 — BODY
  "How does your body feel right now?"
  
  [Illustration: wilting → growing → thriving]
  [5 tap targets with simple visual states, no numbers]
  
Card 2 — PEOPLE  
  "The people around you — helping or draining?"
  
  [Illustration: empty → sparse → full]
  [5 tap targets]

Card 3 — MONEY
  "How does money feel?"
  
  [Illustration: sinking → floating → flowing]
  [5 tap targets]

Card 4 — HOME
  "Does where you live work for you?"
  
  [Illustration: cramped → functional → sanctuary]
  [5 tap targets]

Card 5 — GROWTH
  "Are you learning and developing right now?"
  
  [Illustration: stagnant → moving → accelerating]
  [5 tap targets]

Card 6 — JOY
  "When was the last time you felt genuine joy?"
  
  [Illustration: distant → occasional → regular]
  [5 tap targets]

Card 7 — PURPOSE
  "Do you know what you're building toward?"
  
  [Illustration: fog → glimpses → clear path]
  [5 tap targets]

Card 8 — IDENTITY
  "Do you feel like yourself right now?"
  
  [Illustration: fractured → assembling → whole]
  [5 tap targets]
```

### The visual design of each card

- Full-screen card, sand-50 background
- Question at top in Cormorant Garamond, earth-900, generous
- An abstract, warm illustration in the center — NOT clipart, 
  NOT emoji. Think watercolor washes or ink strokes that evoke 
  the spectrum from struggling to thriving. Each dimension has 
  its own visual language.
- 5 tap targets at bottom — NOT numbered. Labeled with words:
  "struggling — getting by — okay — good — thriving"
  Or even just visual weight — small dot to large dot
- Tapping animates the card forward to the next one
- A subtle shape preview builds in the corner as they go — 
  each tap adds a vertex to an evolving radar form
- The whole sequence takes 60-90 seconds

### What happens at card 8

The shape is complete. It fills the screen.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                         │
│            Your life right now           │
│                                         │
│                  Body                    │
│                   ◇                      │
│           Joy ◇     ◇ People             │
│                  ╲ ╱                     │
│        Purpose ◇──◇──◇ Money             │
│                  ╱ ╲                     │
│         Growth ◇     ◇ Home              │
│                   ◇                      │
│                Identity                  │
│                                         │
│  [The shape — unique to them]            │
│                                         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

The shape is the first thing they own in HUMA. It's THEIRS. 
It looks like nobody else's. And it tells a story they haven't 
heard before.

### The Structural Insight (the aha moment)

Below the shape, HUMA delivers the structural insight — NOT a 
response to what they typed (they didn't type anything), but 
a reading of the RELATIONSHIP between dimensions.

This is generated by a Claude API call that receives the 8 scores 
and produces an insight based on:

1. The most unusual aspect of the shape (what's surprising 
   about this configuration?)
2. The strongest coupling (which dimensions are pulling on 
   each other most?)
3. The hidden asset (which high dimension could support a 
   low dimension?)
4. The one lever (if you could only change one dimension, 
   which would cascade most?)

Example output:

"You rated People at 4 and Identity at 4, but Money at 1 
and Body at 2. That's unusual — most people in financial stress 
feel isolated and lose their sense of self. You haven't.

That means you have something most people in your situation 
don't: a community that still sees you, and a sense of who 
you are that isn't defined by your bank account.

Your strongest lever right now is Body — not because fitness 
solves money problems, but because your energy is the 
infrastructure for everything else. When Body is at 2, you 
can't think clearly enough to solve the money problem even 
if the answer is right in front of you.

The one thing: 20 minutes of movement tomorrow morning. 
Not to get fit. To get your thinking back."

THAT is an insight ChatGPT cannot produce from "I can't sleep." 
It comes from seeing the whole shape and understanding dimensional 
relationships.

### After the insight

Two paths:

**"Tell me more about this" →** Opens a SHORT conversation 
(5-10 minutes) where HUMA asks about the specific coupling 
it identified. Not all 8 dimensions. Not the full 6-phase 
conversation. Just the 2-3 dimensions where the leverage is.

This conversation IS layer 1 of the Design process. HUMA is 
accumulating context. But the operator experiences it as a 
natural follow-up to the shape, not as "phase 1 of 6."

**"I'll come back" →** HUMA saves the shape and says: 
"Your shape is saved. Come back tomorrow — I'll check in."

Either path works. The operator has already received value 
(the structural insight). The shape is the hook that brings 
them back — they want to see if it changes.

## THE DAILY PULSE (Days 2+, 15-30 seconds)

### What it is

The operator returns. HUMA shows their shape from yesterday, 
then asks: "Quick update — anything shifted?"

NOT 8 sliders. Just the shape, with tappable vertices. 
They tap any vertex that changed and adjust it. Most days, 
they tap 0-2 vertices. Done.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                         │
│  Wednesday, March 20                    │
│                                         │
│            [Their shape]                 │
│                                         │
│  Tap anything that shifted              │
│                                         │
│  [Done — nothing changed]               │
│                                         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

If they tap Body (currently at 2), a quick selector appears:
"Body — how's it today?" with the same 5 visual states. 
Tap, done. Shape updates.

15 seconds. Maybe 30 if two things shifted.

### Why this works

The shape IS the daily check-in interface. They're not filling 
out a form. They're LOOKING at their life and updating what 
changed. The shape teaches them to see in systems without 
ever saying the word "systems."

After a week, they know their shape by heart. They can feel 
when a vertex is off. They start NOTICING dimensions they 
used to ignore. "My Joy has been at 2 for six days. I haven't 
done anything creative in two weeks." They didn't need HUMA 
to tell them. The shape made it visible.

## THE PATTERN INSIGHT (Days 3+, passive)

### What it is

After 3-4 days of shape data, HUMA starts seeing temporal 
patterns — not structural (which dimensions are high/low) 
but dynamic (which dimensions MOVE together, which days are 
worse, what predicts what).

These insights appear AFTER the daily pulse, not instead of it.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                         │
│  💡 I noticed something                  │
│                                         │
│  Your Body dips every day after your    │
│  Money drops. Not the other way around. │
│  The money stress hits your body before │
│  you feel it physically.                │
│                                         │
│  [Interesting]  [That's off]            │
│                                         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Maximum one insight every 2-3 days. Not every day. 
Scarcity makes them more powerful. The operator looks 
forward to them because they don't come every time.

Each insight is the coherence recognition moment in miniature. 
"Oh... THAT'S why." The feeling that makes you come back.

### What HUMA is actually doing

Under the surface, HUMA is building a temporal model of this 
person's life. Which dimensions lead, which follow. What the 
delays are. What the coupling strengths are. This is the field 
dynamics model — but the operator just experiences it as 
"HUMA noticed something interesting about my week."

## THE ONE-THING (Days 5+)

### What it is

Once HUMA has enough data to identify the current lever 
(which dimension, if moved, would cascade most positively 
across the others), it starts suggesting one daily action.

This appears after the pulse and any insight:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                         │
│  The one thing today:                   │
│                                         │
│  20 minutes of movement this morning.   │
│  Not to get fit — to get your thinking  │
│  back. Your best ideas come after you   │
│  move.                                  │
│                                         │
│  [Got it]  [Not today]                  │
│                                         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Note: the action is explained in terms of the COUPLING, 
not the dimension. Not "exercise because your Body is low." 
But "move because your thinking improves after movement, 
and clear thinking is what solves the money problem."

That's the one-thing card from the previous spec, but now 
it's grounded in actual dimensional data and temporal patterns — 
not generated from a single text input.

### "Got it" / "Not today"

Same as before. "Got it" → noted, canvas warms slightly. 
"Not today" → no guilt, the suggestion adapts.

But now HUMA can track: did they do the thing? Did the 
relevant dimension shift the next day? If yes, the coupling 
model strengthens. If not, HUMA learns that this lever 
doesn't work for this person and tries a different approach.

## THE MAP CONVERSATION (Week 2-3, earned)

### When it triggers

After 10-14 days of daily shaping, HUMA has:
- A rich temporal model of the operator's dimensions
- Pattern insights that have been confirmed/denied
- A record of which one-things worked and which didn't
- The operator's trust (they've experienced value daily)

HUMA invites:

"I've been watching your shape for two weeks. I think I see 
the whole picture now — not just how things are, but WHY they're 
that way and what could change. Want to map it together? Takes 
about 15-20 minutes."

### How it's different from the current conversation

The 6-phase structure still exists internally. But:

1. **Phase 1 (Ikigai) is shorter** — HUMA already knows their 
   shape, their strongest dimensions, their patterns. It starts 
   with: "Your Identity and People are your strongest dimensions. 
   Tell me about that. What do you love? What are you good at?"

2. **Phase 3 (Situation) is informed** — HUMA already has 2 weeks 
   of dimensional data. It doesn't need to ask "how does money 
   feel?" It says: "Your Money has been at 1-2 for two weeks, 
   and it dips further every Thursday. What happens on Thursdays?"

3. **Phase 4 (Patterns) is grounded** — HUMA has already tested 
   one-things and knows what works for this person. The pattern 
   recommendations build on what's already been tried, not 
   starting from scratch.

4. **The whole conversation is 15-20 minutes** instead of 40, 
   because half the discovery already happened through the 
   daily shaping.

### After the conversation

The full canvas appears (three-tab experience):
- **Your Map:** Palmer-aligned spatial canvas with warmth
- **Your Day:** One-thing card (now powered by full canvas data)
- **Your Journey:** Shape timeline (now they have 2 weeks of 
  daily shapes + the first post-conversation shape)

The transition from daily shaping to the full three-tab 
experience IS the "glasses moment" — but it's earned, not 
given. They've been building toward it for two weeks.

## THE DAILY EXPERIENCE (Post-Canvas)

After the map conversation, the daily experience evolves:

### Morning (30 seconds)

Open HUMA → see the shape → tap anything that shifted → see 
the one-thing card. Same flow as before, but now the one-thing 
is powered by the full canvas: it references specific commitments 
("this connects to 'evenings free by 4'"), specific practices 
("using the Production Batching method"), and specific levers 
identified in the conversation.

### The deeper daily view (optional)

If they want more, they tap "See my full day" and get the 
timeline with all their time blocks, WHY chains, and HOW 
(compiled pattern methods). Available. Never forced.

### The weekly planner (advanced, optional)

"Plan my week" → the 7-column interactive grid. For power 
users who want to design at the weekly level. Never the default.

## THE WEEKLY SHAPE MOMENT (Sundays)

### What it is

Not a "review." A MOMENT. HUMA shows the operator their 
shape evolution for the past week — a subtle animation 
morphing from Monday's shape to today's shape.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                         │
│  Your week:                             │
│                                         │
│  [animated shape morph: Mon → Sun]      │
│                                         │
│  Body:    2.0 → 2.8  ↑                 │
│  Money:   1.5 → 1.5  ─                 │
│  Joy:     1.0 → 2.0  ↑                 │
│                                         │
│  What I noticed:                        │
│  Your Body improved every day after     │
│  you did the morning movement. Joy      │
│  followed. Money held steady — which    │
│  is actually good news. It means the    │
│  stress isn't getting worse even as     │
│  other things improve.                  │
│                                         │
│  Coherence: 41 → 48  (+7)              │
│                                         │
│  [Sounds right]  [Tell me more]         │
│                                         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

The animated shape morph is the signature HUMA moment. 
Watching your shape change over a week — even slightly — 
is visceral. It's the proof that what you're doing matters.

### "Tell me more" (deeper review)

Opens the full validation review: tap-to-answer for each 
commitment, systemic insight, one adjustment suggestion. 
Available for those who want it. The default weekly 
experience is the shape morph + one insight. 60 seconds.

## THE SHAPE AS GROWTH ENGINE

### What people share

Not the canvas. Not the spatial diagram. **The shape at two 
points in time.**

```
     January            Now
       ◇                 ◇
      /|\               /|\ 
     ◇ ◇ ◇            ◇ ◇ ◇
      \|/               \|/ 
       ◇                 ◇
       
  Coherence: 38 → 67
```

One image. Dark background. Sage shapes. 
Sized for Instagram (1080×1080) and Twitter (1200×630).

This is the growth engine. User shares shape → friend says 
"what is this" → friend builds their shape (90 seconds) → 
friend gets their structural insight → friend comes back → 
friend shares their shape.

**The shape is the virus.** Not because it's gamified. Because 
it's genuinely interesting. Everyone's shape is different. 
Everyone wants to see theirs. And everyone wants to see how 
it changes.

### "People like you" signals

"73% of people with a shape like yours found that starting 
with Body made everything else easier."

"This practice (Morning Anchoring) is working for people 
with your shape. Most see Body improve within 2 weeks."

Initially from simulation data. Over time, from real 
aggregated data. Always anonymous. Always social.

## WHY THIS WORKS FOR THE 97%

The previous critique said most people won't examine their 
lives. That's true if "examining your life" means sitting 
down for 40 minutes and talking about your purpose.

But building your shape? That's 90 seconds. That's curiosity. 
That's "I wonder what mine looks like." There's no vulnerability 
required. No deep conversation. No commitment. Just tap 8 
questions and see your shape.

The insight that follows is surprising — not because the person 
asked for it, but because the shape revealed something they 
didn't know about themselves. The Spotify Wrapped model: 
the data was always there. The tool just made it visible.

Then the daily pulse is 15 seconds of updating the shape. 
Not a journal. Not a self-improvement exercise. Just: "did 
anything change?" Tap, done.

The one-thing card is one action. Not a plan. Not a schedule. 
Not a commitment. One thing. Do it or don't. No guilt.

The weekly shape morph is watching yourself change. Visceral. 
Shareable. The kind of thing you screenshot and send to someone.

At no point does HUMA say "let's examine your life" or 
"test your hypothesis" or "validate your assumptions." 
HUMA says: "here's your shape. Here's what I noticed. Here's 
one thing that might help. Want to see how you're changing?"

The examination happens. The hypothesis testing happens. 
The validation happens. But the operator experiences it as: 
seeing their shape, getting insights, trying one thing, and 
watching themselves grow.

The 97% don't resist self-improvement. They resist the 
FEELING of self-improvement — the homework, the commitment, 
the guilt, the jargon. Make it feel like curiosity instead 
of work, and the paradigm breaks.

## WHAT THIS MEANS FOR THE ARCHITECTURE

### Everything stays:
- RPPL, pattern compilation, 8 capitals
- QoL decomposition (powers the canvas + one-thing selection)
- Validation loop (drives warmth + coherence score)
- Field dynamics model (drives temporal insights)
- 6-phase conversation (shortened, earned at week 2)
- Three-tab structure (Your Map / Your Day / Your Journey)
- Palmer-aligned spatial canvas with warmth
- Graduation engine (passive measurement)
- Ethical framework (dependency test, never blame the person)
- The six innovations (all still valid)

### What changes:
- Entry: Shape Builder (90 sec visual) replaces "What's on your mind?"
- Daily: Shape update (15 sec tap) replaces dimension sliders
- Weekly: Shape morph animation replaces validation review as default
- The 40-minute conversation becomes 15-20 minutes, earned at week 2
- The shape IS the share artifact, the daily interface, AND 
  the analytical framework (radar chart = shape)

### The shape IS the product

The capital radar chart — which we moved off the canvas as 
"analytical" — turns out to BE the product. Not as an 
analytical tool in the Evolve tab. As the primary interface.

The shape is what you build on day 1.
The shape is what you update daily.
The shape is what HUMA reads for insights.
The shape is what morphs weekly to show progress.
The shape is what you share.
The shape is what changes over seasons.

The shape IS HUMA.

## BUILD SEQUENCE

### Phase 1: Shape Builder (3-4 sessions)
- 8 cards, one per dimension
- Visual tap targets (not numbered sliders)
- Shape builds as they go (SVG radar)
- Structural insight generated via Claude API
- "Tell me more" → short conversation
- "I'll come back" → save shape + invite to return
- Auth (magic link) to enable return

### Phase 2: Daily Shape Pulse (2-3 sessions)
- Show yesterday's shape
- Tap to adjust vertices
- "Done — nothing changed" shortcut
- Save to pulse history
- Pattern detection engine (after 3-4 days)
- Insight cards (max one per 2-3 days)

### Phase 3: One-Thing Card (2-3 sessions)
- Generated from dimensional analysis + temporal patterns
- Explained in coupling terms (not just "exercise more")
- Got it / Not today
- Track outcomes vs predictions

### Phase 4: Weekly Shape Morph (2 sessions)
- Animate shape from Monday → Sunday
- Show dimension deltas
- One insight
- Coherence score
- "Tell me more" → deeper review

### Phase 5: Map Conversation (3-4 sessions)
- Triggered at week 2-3
- Uses accumulated shape + pulse data as context
- 15-20 minute version of 6-phase conversation
- Produces full canvas data

### Phase 6: Three-Tab Experience (4-5 sessions)
- Your Map (Palmer canvas with warmth)
- Your Day (one-thing card, full day optional, weekly planner optional)
- Your Journey (shape timeline, seasonal review)

### Phase 7: Shape Sharing (2-3 sessions)
- Generate shape card (dark mode, SVG → PNG)
- Two time points (January → Now)
- Coherence delta
- Share button + Web Share API
- Per-map OG image with shape

USABLE PRODUCT after Phase 4 (~10 sessions).
Shape builder + daily pulse + one-thing + weekly morph.

Ship to real humans. Get feedback. Then build phases 5-7.

## THE TEST

The product works when:

1. Someone builds their shape in 90 seconds and says 
   "...how did it know that?" about the structural insight.

2. They come back the next day to update their shape. 
   Not because they should. Because they want to see 
   if anything changed.

3. On day 4, HUMA shows them a connection between two 
   dimensions they hadn't noticed. They feel the coherence 
   recognition. They're hooked.

4. On day 7, they watch their shape morph from Monday to 
   Sunday. Something moved. They screenshot it.

5. On day 14, HUMA says "I see your whole picture now. 
   Want to map it?" They say yes — not because we asked, 
   but because they're CURIOUS.

6. They text their shape to a friend: "Look at this."

7. The friend opens HUMA. Builds their shape.

The paradigm breaks one shape at a time.
